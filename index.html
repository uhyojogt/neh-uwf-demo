Ningbo Eye Hospital

NEH UWF demonstration data

Twenty de-identified ultra-wide-field fundus images and four-class annotations for demonstrating the fine-tuning workflow accompanying the RRD segmentation-to-surgery study.

These samples demonstrate the data format and fine-tuning workflow. They are not the complete datasets used to train or evaluate the reported models and are not intended for clinical use.

Requesting the complete dataset

The remaining hospital-derived images are subject to institutional ethics, patient-confidentiality and data-governance requirements. Qualified academic researchers may submit a request to 2500671012@mails.szu.edu.cn.

Requests should include the research objectives, analysis plan, requested data fields, institutional affiliation, ethics approval or exemption, data-security arrangements and proposed retention period. Requests are normally evaluated within 30 working days. Approved access is limited to the authorised project and requires a signed data-use or material-transfer agreement.

Integrity and citation

Use checksums_sha256.txt to verify downloaded files. Citation metadata and the permanent dataset identifier will be added to the archival repository record.
